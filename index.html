See what we're working on

Lightspeeed Travel

With aspirations of traversing vast cosmic distances at the speed of light, our efforts in light speed travel focus on theoretical frameworks and propulsion systems capable of achieving such velocities. Challenges include the energy requirements and the impact of relativistic effects on onboard systems and passengers.

  • Find out more

Quantum Computing

Our team is at the forefront of quantum computing research, aiming to harness the immense power of quantum mechanics for computational tasks. Current challenges include overcoming decoherence and maintaining the delicate quantum states required for computation.

  • Find out more

Teleportation

In our pursuit of teleportation technology, we're exploring methods to transmit quantum information instantaneously over long distances. Key hurdles involve overcoming the limitations imposed by quantum entanglement and ensuring the faithful reconstruction of matter at the destination.
